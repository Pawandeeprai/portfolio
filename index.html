ABOUT

I still remember the exact moment I chose to pursue software development. I was taking a break from studying for a Calculus final. I was using this hack to reposition all the monsters in MapleStory onto one spot so I could level up faster. I thought about how awesome it would be if I could make something like that. I really liked that idea, and I decided to go with it. I switched my major to Computer Science, signed up for courses on Treehouse, but I wanted to learn more in a shorter amount of time, so I turned to App Academy.
